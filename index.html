Somali Constraction Company

somConstruction Company is private company which was established in 2010, since its inception, the company has emerged as one of the most respected general contracting firms in Somalia. The company provides wide range of construction types which are not limited to, Residential Houses, Health Centers, Shopping Malls, Educational Facilities, worshiping and etc. som has since completed and delivered various infrastructure projects, program management services, and architectural and engineering services for various companies, agencies and Diaspora community throughout Somalia. som has worked in every district within Muqdisho and understands how to successfully complete projects within a difficult and logistically challenging region.
